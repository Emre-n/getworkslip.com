We collect the following types of information:

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with:

You are responsible for obtaining any necessary consent from your customers before entering their personal information (name, email, address, signature) into Workslip. You represent that you have the right to share this information with us for the purpose of providing the Service.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. To exercise these rights, contact us at support@getworkslip.com.

8. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed to children under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we learn we have collected such information, we will delete it promptly.
